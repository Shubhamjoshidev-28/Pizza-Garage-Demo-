# Introduction

The Restaurant Ordering System allows customers to browse your menu, place orders, and track their order status.

Restaurant staff can manage incoming orders, update the menu, and edit business information through the Admin Dashboard.

---

# Customer Website

Customers can:

* Browse the menu
* View item descriptions
* Select different sizes (if available)
* Add items to the cart
* Enter their name
* Place an order
* Track active orders
* View previous orders

No customer account or password is required.

---

# Admin Dashboard

The Admin Dashboard is used to manage the restaurant.

After logging in, the dashboard provides three sections:

* Orders
* Menu
* Settings

---

# Orders

The Orders page displays all customer orders.

Each order includes:

* Order ID
* Customer Name
* Ordered Items
* Total Amount
* Payment Status
* Order Status
* Order Time

Restaurant staff can update:

* Payment Status
* Order Status

Available order statuses:

* Accepted
* Preparing
* Ready to Collect
* Delivered

Once an order is marked as Delivered, it moves to the customer's Order History.

---

# Menu Management

The Menu page allows you to manage all food and beverage items.

You can:

* Add new items
* Edit existing items
* Delete items
* Enable or disable menu items
* Set prices
* Configure multiple sizes
* Upload or change image links

Every saved change is immediately available to customers after they refresh the website.

---

# Business Settings

The Settings page stores your business information.

You can update:

* Business Name
* Phone Number
* Email Address
* Business Address
* Opening Hours
* WhatsApp Number
* Instagram Link
* Facebook Link
* Logo URL

After saving, the customer website automatically reflects the updated information.

---

# Customer Workflow

The normal customer experience is:

1. Open the website.
2. Browse the menu.
3. Add items to the cart.
4. Enter a name when prompted.
5. Place the order.
6. Track the order status.
7. Collect the order.
8. View the completed order in Order History.

---

# Best Practices

* Keep menu prices updated.
* Remove unavailable items instead of deleting them when possible.
* Update order statuses promptly so customers receive accurate information.
* Verify payment status before marking an order as Delivered.
* Keep business information current.

---

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Why can't customers see a new menu item?

Ask the customer to refresh the website.

---

## Why isn't an order appearing?

Check the Orders page and refresh the dashboard.

---

## Can I temporarily hide a menu item?

Yes. Mark the item as unavailable instead of deleting it.

---

## Can I change business contact information?

Yes. Open the Settings page, update the information, and save the changes.

---

## Do customers need to create an account?

No. Customers only need to provide their name when placing an order.

---

# Troubleshooting

## Orders are not updating

Refresh the Orders page.

---

## Menu changes are not visible

Refresh the customer website.

---

## Unable to log in

Verify that you are using the correct administrator password.

---

## Incorrect business information

Update the information from the Settings page and save the changes.

---

# Tips

* Review new orders frequently during business hours.
* Keep the menu organized by category.
* Remove discontinued items from the menu.
* Update opening hours during holidays or special events.
* Test the ordering process after making major menu changes.
